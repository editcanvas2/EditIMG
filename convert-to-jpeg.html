How to Convert an Image to JPEG

  1. Upload Your Image: Drag and drop any common image file (like PNG, WEBP, or BMP) onto the upload area, or click to select a file.
  2. Set JPEG Quality: Use the "Quality" slider to choose the desired quality for your output JPEG file. Higher quality means a larger file size.
  3. Process the Image: Click the "Process Image" button to start the conversion.
  4. Download Your JPEG: Click the "Download Image" button to save the new .jpg file to your device.

JPEG Conversion FAQ

JPEG (or JPG) is the most widely supported image format on the web and across devices. It uses lossy compression to create much smaller file sizes than formats like PNG, making it perfect for photos, web graphics, and for sharing via email.

The JPEG format does not support transparency. When you convert a PNG with a transparent background to JPEG, the transparent areas will typically be filled with a solid color, usually white.
